Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

My K-Name is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

1.1 Locally Stored Data

The following data is stored only on your device using SharedPreferences:

  • Favorite Names: Korean names you've marked as favorites
  • App Preferences: Your onboarding status and app settings
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, emails, or any personally identifiable information

1.2 Advertising Data

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device advertising ID
  • IP address
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Ad interaction data

For more information about how Google uses this data, please review:

  • Google's Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob Partners

2. How We Use Information

The app uses information only to:

  • Display Korean name recommendations based on your selections (gender, personality, interests, zodiac, blood type)
  • Save your favorite names locally on your device
  • Provide text-to-speech pronunciation for Korean names
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All your preferences and favorite names are stored locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences
  • No Cloud Sync: Your data is never transmitted to our servers
  • Data Removal: Uninstalling the app removes all stored data from your device
  • No User Accounts: The app does not require account creation or login

4. Permissions

The app requires the following permissions:

  • INTERNET: Required to load advertisements and Google Image Search feature
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Required to check network connectivity for ads

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Google AdMob

This app displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob is a third-party advertising service that may collect and use data as described in Google's Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by:

  • Visiting Google Ad Settings
  • Using your device's privacy settings to limit ad tracking

5.2 Google Image Search

The app includes a feature to search famous people with Korean names using Google Image Search. When you use this feature, you will be directed to Google's website, which is subject to Google's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your information to third parties, except:

  • As required by law
  • To Google AdMob for advertising purposes (as described in Section 5.1)

7. Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require any personal information input.

However, please note that advertisements displayed through Google AdMob are subject to Google's policies regarding children's privacy and COPPA compliance.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data stored on your device through Android's app data settings
  • Delete all app data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in device settings
  • Opt out of personalized advertising through device settings or Google Ad Settings
